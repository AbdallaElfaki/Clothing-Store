About Us

Les Rock Clothing was founded in 1986 by Jim Lauren and Jason Holmes. They intended to provide people with a store that made it easier for people to shop for business attire colors. Instead of hoping that a clothing store has a certain blouse or shirt that you like in black or white, you can be certain that Les Rocks Clothing will have it. They use a wide range of designs in order to attract customers to their store.



Trusted by Macy's, Nordstrom Rack, Target, and Walmart
